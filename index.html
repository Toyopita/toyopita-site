ISOMETRIC VIEW · NTS · OSAKA STUDIO

An all-rounder, working out of Osaka.

Born in Osaka in 1992, Toyopita began his musical journey in 2019. A true all-rounder, he handles every aspect of music production from track-making to mastering.

Deeply inspired by Jamaica's dub culture and the free-spirited expression of reggae music, his approach to music creation is both innovative and deeply rooted in tradition. Captivated by the art of transforming existing tracks and the techniques at the root of remixing, this fascination drives his unique production style — further shaped by influences from hip-hop, R&B, and techno.
