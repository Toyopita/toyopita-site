Privacy Notice.

— EFFECTIVE APR. MMXXVI · TOY-002 —

This site (toyopita.com) does not collect personally identifying information about its visitors. There are no sign-ups, no comment sections, and no logged-in user accounts. The pages are static HTML.

— 01Analytics

The site uses Cloudflare Web Analytics for aggregate visitor statistics. Cloudflare Web Analytics is a privacy-first service:

  • It does not use cookies or local storage.
  • It does not collect or store IP addresses.
  • It does not track individuals across sites.
  • Only aggregate metrics are reported (page views, country, referrer, browser).

More information about how Cloudflare handles this data is available at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

— 02Hosting & Logs

The site is delivered through Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may retain technical request logs for short periods for the purposes of security, abuse prevention, and infrastructure operation. These logs are not accessible to or used by the site owner for any tracking purpose.

— 03Embedded Content

The site embeds the official Spotify artist player. When you interact with the embedded player, Spotify's own privacy policy applies. See Spotify Privacy Policy. Outbound links to Apple Music, Instagram, and SoundCloud are similarly governed by their respective privacy policies once you click through.

— 04Email Inquiries

If you contact the studio at toyopitawhich@gmail.com, your email address and the contents of your message are received and stored for the purpose of replying. The information is not shared with any third party and is not used for marketing.

— 05Your Rights

If you believe the site has any information about you and would like it removed, please write to toyopitawhich@gmail.com. We will respond promptly.

— 06Changes

This notice may be revised. Material changes will be reflected in the revision tag at the top of this page (currently REV.A) and dated.
